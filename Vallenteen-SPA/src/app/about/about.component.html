Hello, my name is Sergiu Valentin and I am an independent electronic music producer

Born in Serbia, and currently based in Romania where I study to become an engineer in computer science. making music has been one of my many passions ever since 2011, when I first got my hands on a music software because I wanted so badly to produce just like Basshunter, my then favourite artist.
Since then I changed my artist name a bunch of times, the genre I produced, techniques, but the excitement I get from developing an idea into a full-length musical creation and sharing it with you still stays the same.

About my music

The music I create spans over quite a handful of subgenres of the electronic music. often blending between lo-fi hip hop, house, or bedroom pop, the genre itself in my opinion is not so relevant. Instead, I always aim to faithfully depict an emotion or a situation from the daily life.
All situations that inspire me to create are never random, it is always something more or less ordinary from the daily life. This is all because I try my best to make you, dear listener, relate to my music, identify with it, and remind you of the simple beautiful things in life.
